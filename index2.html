My Project

In the United States more people are incarated at a higher rate than anywhere else in the world. With 2,220,300 adults in federal and state prisons according to the US Bureau of Justice Statistics (BJS), the U.S. has become the breedng ground of corporal punishment. The census from state and federal correctional facilities show that there are 1,719 state prisons and 102 federal prisons, which supported by a Prison Policy Initiative briefing released in 2014. This project intends to map the state felony restrictions and voter ID laws that keep this system thriving as well as the use maps to showcase where some of these prisons are in relation to congressional districts.
